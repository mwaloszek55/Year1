Welcome to Bloodelf Escapade!

Your goal is to eliminate all Bloodelfs with your nifty gun-laser thingy.

After you have defeated all Bloodelfs, your next challenger approaches - their Boss!

You can use Arrow Keys in order to move, hit spacebar to shoot.

Gain score by killing enemies. Your score is multiplied by the amount of amunition you have left, your hp and time.

After the end of the game, score is automatically recorded, however, only the top 10 players with the most points show up! :)
